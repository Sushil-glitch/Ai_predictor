About the Project

The Student Success Predictor is an end-to-end data science application demo designed to empower educators and students alike.

Empowering Academic Excellence

The Student Success Predictor is a dedicated platform designed to bridge the gap between data and academic achievement. Our mission is to provide educators, students, and institutional leaders with the predictive insights needed to foster a supportive and successful learning environment.

What We Offer

Performance Prediction

Sophisticated data modeling allows students to understand how their current study patterns and backgrounds influence their projected outcomes, enabling early course corrections.

Retention Support

By identifying behavioral and academic risk factors early, our system helps prevent dropouts before they happen, ensuring every student has a pathway to graduation.

Why Use This Platform?

Education is more than just grades—it's about progress. This website serves as a tool for transparency, allowing users to visualize trends through our live dashboard and take proactive steps toward improved performance. Whether you are a student looking to optimize your study hours or a teacher monitoring class-wide trends, our insights make a difference.
